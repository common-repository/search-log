Keep track of every search visitors do on your site so that you can make sure they're finding what they're looking for.  

== Description ==

A simple way to keep track of what your visitors are searching for. This plugin logs every search your visitors make so that you can know what they're looking for and make sure they're finding it.

No complicated setup or mess of settings.  Just activate and you're done!

The plugin adds a menu item in your WordPress admin panel titled "Search Logs".  This page gives you a display of the most recent searches as well as the most popular searches your visitors are making.  This will allow you to know what your readers are looking for so you can make sure the searches are providing meaningful results.  Check out the screenshots to see an example of what it looks like.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `search-log.php` and `search-log.png` to the `/wp-content/plugins/search-log/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

OR

Just grab the Search Log plugin from the built in plugin directory and choose "Install now", hit ok, then select the "Activate Plugin" link.
